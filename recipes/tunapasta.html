Quick and Easy Tuna Pasta

This delicious tuna pasta is perfect for a quick and easy dinner. It's flavorful, satisfying, and can be made in under 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces pasta (penne or fusilli)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 can (5 ounces) tuna, drained
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup pitted black olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and onion, and saut√© until the onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the tuna to the skillet and break it up with a spoon. Cook for 2-3 minutes until heated through.
  4. Add the cherry tomatoes and olives to the skillet. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the tomatoes start to soften.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to combine. Add the fresh parsley, red pepper flakes (if using), salt, and pepper. Toss again to mix everything well.
  6. Serve the tuna pasta hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
